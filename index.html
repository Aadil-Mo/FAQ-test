Frequently Asked Questions

AquaPure is a fictional brand of reusable, insulated water bottles designed to keep drinks cold or hot for extended periods while being eco-friendly.

Thanks to its double-wall vacuum insulation, AquaPure bottles can keep beverages cold for up to 24 hours.

We recommend hand-washing your AquaPure bottle with warm, soapy water to preserve the exterior finish and ensure longevity. The lid may be top-rack dishwasher safe.

Currently, AquaPure is a concept for portfolio purposes and is not available for purchase. You can simulate ordering on the landing page example!
